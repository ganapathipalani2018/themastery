## 1. **Purpose**

To provide job seekers with a fast, intuitive, and customizable platform to build professional, ATS-compliant resumes and cover letters using AI-enhanced guidance, industry-specific templates, and career-focused suggestions.

---

## 2. **Scope**

This platform will allow users to:

* Create, edit, and manage resumes
* Generate cover letters based on resumes
* Export documents in PDF/Word formats
* Receive resume tips and AI-powered suggestions
* Store multiple versions
* Optionally subscribe for full feature access

---

| Persona                        | Description                                 |

| **Experienced Professional**   | Mid-career individual updating resume       |
| **Career Switcher**            | Moving to a new industry                    |
